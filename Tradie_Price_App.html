How Pricing Works

Regular Rate — First job on site. Includes the call-out fee. Use this for the first item you quote.

Add-On Rate — Extra work on the same visit. No call-out fee included. Higher margin for you.

Discounted Rate — 15% off regular rate. Includes call-out. Use for members, promotions or real estate clients.

Discounted Add-On — 15% off add-on rate. No call-out. For extra discounted work on same visit.

Tip — Smart Pricing Flow
Job 1 → Regular Rate
Each extra job same visit → Add-On Rate
Regular customers → Discounted Rate
